Welcome to Sigweje High School

Sigweje High School, established in 1996, is located in Ladysmith, KwaZulu-Natal near GG Shopping Centre. We serve Grade 8 to Grade 12 learners, fostering academic excellence and cultural pride.

Academics

We offer a strong curriculum and a dedicated staff that supports academic growth and exam success.

Sport Success

Our students shine in athletics, football, netball, and more — building discipline and teamwork.

Cultural Activities

Drama, music, heritage days — our learners engage in vibrant cultural expression throughout the year.
